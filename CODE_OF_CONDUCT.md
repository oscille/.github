# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for
everyone who contributes to or participates in Oscille projects, regardless of
background or identity.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory or derogatory
comments, personal attacks, and publishing others' private information.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project
maintainers at **conduct@oscille.com**.
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
